Gachon ConvNet Challenge

The "Gachon ConvNet Challenge" is a team project in the MultimediaLab class. Each team (4 ~ 5 students) will be given a specific task, in which, they will employ deep learning model to achieve results. The challenge is divided into three parts: Training phase, testing phase and Presentation. The key summary of the challenge is as follows:

  • For each task, the training and validation datasets will be provided. The students are restricted to use the provided datasets only. For dataset, training procedure and working environment, please refer to sections 4 and 5.
  • After the training period, each team will be provided with test images. The teams will test their models for the test images to obtain output images. The output images have to be submitted to 'evaluation server' within due date.
  • All output images will undergo some quality evaluation metrics (please refer to section 7) and based on the results, the leader-board will be set. The leader-board will consists of all teams' performances according to the evaluation metrics in a descending manner, i.e. the best team will be at the top of the leader-board, the second best team will be at the second position and so on.
  • All teams are required to present their solutions within due date. The presentation should briey contain (not limited to) problem statement, solution, contribution (if any) and summary.
  • All teams are required to write a final presentation report on the solution and submit within due date.
